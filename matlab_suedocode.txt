attractive force: F = G*M*m/r^2

G = 6.674 * 10^-11 N*m^2/kg^2
M = 1 kg
m = 1 kg

a(r) = r_double_dot - r*theta_dot^2 = G*M*m/r^2
a(theta) = r*theta_double_dot + 2*r_dot*theta_dot = 0

r_double_dot = G*M*m/r^2 + r*theta_dot^2
theta_double_dot = -2*r_dot*theta_dot/r

n(1) = r		n(1)_dot = n(2)
n(2) = r_dot		n(2)_dot = r_double_dot
n(3) = theta		n(3)_dot = n(4)
n(4) = theta_dot	n(4) = theta_double_dot

x = r*cos(theta)
y = r*sin(theta)
